Help & Information

This web application streams your primary monitor screen. You can control the frame rate using the slider below the stream.

Controls:

  • Stop Stream: Click this button to stop the screen streaming.
  • FPS Slider: Adjust the frame rate of the stream.
  • ? Opens this help modal with information about the app.

If you have any other questions, feel free to reach out to support.
